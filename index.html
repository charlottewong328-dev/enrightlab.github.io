About

The Enright lab seeks to develop a spectroscopic understanding of colloidal nanomaterial systems for sustainable energy solutions. Our group uses nanomaterials to convert and manipulate sunlight.

We explore photocatalysis for renewable fuel generation and luminescent solar concentrators for power-generating windows — semi-transparent, tinted panels that convert light into electricity for buildings.
